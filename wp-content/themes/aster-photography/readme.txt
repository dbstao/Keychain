== Description ==

The Aster Photography WordPress Theme is an ideal solution tailored for photographers and photography businesses looking to showcase their work in a visually stunning and professional manner. Whether you're involved in portrait photography, wedding photography, or commercial photography, this theme is designed to elevate your online portfolio. With its clean, minimalist layout, the Aster Photography WordPress Theme emphasises your images, allowing visitors to fully appreciate your photography services. It’s perfect for freelance photographers, event photographers, or those offering photography packages, giving you the flexibility to display your photography pricing, rates, and custom services. Additionally, Aster Photography WordPress Theme is versatile enough to cater to a wide range of photography niches, including nature photography, landscape photography, real estate photography, and fashion photography. With customizable features like personalised galleries, photo sliders, and testimonial sections, you can create an impressive portfolio that reflects your unique style. The theme is fully responsive, ensuring your photography portfolio looks great on all devices, from desktop to mobile. Additional features include SEO optimization, fast loading times, and integration with social media to boost your online presence. Whether you're a seasoned professional or just starting your photography business, the Aster Photography WordPress Theme is the perfect tool for showcasing your talent and growing your brand.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload Theme and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does this theme support any plugins? =

Yes, the theme is integrated to work with following plugins:
* One Click Demo Import: This plugin is recommended to use for demo importing purpose.
* GiveWP – Donation Plugin and Fundraising Platform: This plugin is recommended to use for adding donation forms and accepting donations.
* Contact Form 7: This plugin is recommended to use for contact forms on contact page.
